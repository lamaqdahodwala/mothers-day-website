5 reasons we love ma

1.

She is incredibly fun

Ma is incredibly fun, with a smile that can light up a room. She never passes up the chance to go somewhere new, or do something fun.

2.

She is hardworking

Ma is incredibly hardworking. She commits in every single task, and does not stop until it is done properly, which is one of the most inspiring parts about her.

3.

She knows how to make you laugh

No matter what, the most defining part about Ma is her ability to make everyone laugh without limit. A simple look from her can make anyone crack up.

4.

She never gives up

Ma's sheer strength to never give up, even at impossible tasks, is one of the most impressive parts about her. No one alive, dead, or unborn could even compete with the raw willpower that Ma has.

5.

She forces all these principles on you

Even with how incredible Ma is, she never ceases an opportunity to force what makes her good onto you. The pleasure for her comes from the fact that she helped you out of a problem, or rough spot.
